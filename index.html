Oven Roasted Potatoes

Perfectly crisp buttery potatoes ready in seconds

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Chop potatoes
  2. Coat potatoes in olive oil
  3. Add Italian seasoning
  4. Bake at 400 for 15-20 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and add salt
